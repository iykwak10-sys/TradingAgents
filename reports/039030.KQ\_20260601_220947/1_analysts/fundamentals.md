## 1. Company Profile and Business Overview
EO Technics Co., Ltd. is a leading South Korean technology company specializing in semiconductor equipment and materials. The company is primarily known for its laser-based equipment used in various stages of semiconductor manufacturing, including marking, cutting, and drilling. As the semiconductor industry shifts toward advanced packaging (HBM, Chiplets) and smaller process nodes, EO Technics’ laser solutions have become increasingly critical.

## 2. Financial Performance Analysis

- **Revenue Growth:** Quarterly revenue grew from **84.82 billion KRW** in March 2025 to **115.10 billion KRW** in March 2026, a year-over-year increase of approximately **35.7%**.
- **Profitability:** Net Income for the most recent quarter (Q1 2026) reached **31.21 billion KRW**, compared to **14.41 billion KRW** in Q1 2025. This represents more than a **116% increase** in quarterly profit.
- **Margins:** The TTM Operating Margin stands at a healthy **25.89%**, with a Profit Margin of **18%**. These margins indicate strong pricing power and operational efficiency in a specialized high-tech market.

- **Investing Activities:** The company is actively investing, with **45.08 billion KRW** in investing cash outflows in the last quarter, primarily directed toward financial assets and property, plant, and equipment (PPE).
